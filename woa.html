This is an the second studio album by the Nigerian Singer ASAKE who is a signee of OLAMIDE'S record label YBNL .It was released on Friday,June 16,2023 through YBNL Nation and Empire Distribution, Preceded by the release of the singles "Yoga", "2:30" and "Amapiano".

It comprises of 14 tracks which comprises of a collaboration with his record label boss OLAMIDE only.
Its production was handled by Magicsticks,ragge and some other producers.
