Written by Kenez

Born in the Wild is the debut studio album by Nigerian singer Tems. It was released on 7 June 2024, via RCA Records and Since '93. The album was supported by the singles Me & U, Love Me JeJe and Burning. Born in the Wild features guest appearances from ASAKE and foreign rapper J COLE. Its production was handled by Tems, GuiltyBeatz, Sarz, P2J, London, Spax, and DameDame. In promotion of the album, Tems will embark on the Born in the Wild Tour. The album was a commercial success reaching the top thirty in the UK, Netherlands and Switzerland. In the US, it peaked at fifty-six becoming the highest peak for an album by a Nigerian female artist. It consists of 18 tracks
