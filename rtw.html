This is the debut studio album by Nigerian Singer-songwriter RUGER, a signee of the Jonzing record label .It was released on September 1st,2023.
It comprises of 14 tracks which comprises of collaborations with STEFFLON DON,JUGGLERZ,PROJEXX,SAUTI SOLandGOVANA. Its production was handled by JUGGLERZ,KUKURUKU BEATZ and PROJEXX. The production was handled by Kukbeatz, Cadenza, TSB, Legendury Beatz, BenjiFlow, TheKidParris, Jugglerz and Frnkie
