Written by Kenez

Exceedingly gifted Nigerian vocalist and songwriter who's currently signed under Mavins Record, Ayra Starr, has just unveiled her highly anticipated sophomore album known as “TYIT21 (The Year I Turned 21) Album.” Ayra Starr, a rising star in Nigerian music and signee of Mavins Record, marks important moments in her life through her music. At 19, she released her first album, “19 & Dangerous,” reflecting her talent and potential at that age. Now, at 21, she celebrates her growth with her second album, “TYIT21 (The Year I Turned 21) Album.” Through her songs, she shares her experiences and emotions, inviting listeners to join her journey into adulthood. With her unique voice and captivating melodies, Ayra Starr continues to impress her dedicated fans and music lovers worldwide.

Furthermore, this second work of art features a total of 14 astounding track that are certain to electrify her fans and listeners. This new project presents a total of six collaborations with renowned and award-winning singers including ASAKE, COCO JONES and GIVEON
