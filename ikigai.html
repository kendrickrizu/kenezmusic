Ikigai / 生き甲斐 (Vol. 1) is an EP by Nigerian rapper Olamide, released on 26 June 2024. It follows his 2023 album Unruly. It comprises of 7 tracks which comprises of collaborations with ASAKE,FIREBOY DML and YBNL alumni PHEELZ, LIL KESH, YOUNG JOHN
THE PLAYLIST OF THE ALBUM IS-

  1. METAVERSE
  2. UPTOWN DISCO [ft ASAKE,FIREBOY DML]
  3. MAKAVELI
  4. KNOCKOUT [ft SABRI]
  5. HELLO HABIBI
  6. MOROWORE
  7. SYNCHRO SYSTEM [ft PHEELZ, YOUNG JOHN, LIL KESH]
