Written by Kenez

This is the seventh studio album by Nigerian singer Burna Boy. It was released on 25 August 2023, through Atlantic, Spaceship Records, and Bad Habit.
It comprises of 14 tracks which comprises of collaborations with GZA, SEYI VIBEZ, 21 SAVAGE, DAVE, RZA, J COLE and BYRON MESSIA. The album was preceded by four singles, "Sittin' on Top of the World", "Talibans II", "Big 7", and "Cheat on Me".
