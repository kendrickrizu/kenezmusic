Written by Kenez

This is the tenth studio album by Nigerian rapper,Singer and YBNL record label boss OLAMIDE .It was released on AUGUST 9,2023 by YBNL Nation and Empire Distribution.
It comprises of 14 tracks which comprises of collaborations with CKAY,BNXN,ASAKE,FIREBOY DML and REMA and featured production from Niketaz, P.Priime, LNK, Eskeez, Bbanks, Alex Lustig, CKay and Magicsticks. Unruly was preceded by the singles "New Religion" and "Trumpet". The album serves as a follow-up to UY Scuti
