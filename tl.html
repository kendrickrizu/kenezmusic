Written by Kenez

This is the fourth studio album by Nigerian Singer Davido .It was released on March 2,2023,through Davido Music Worldwide,Columbia Records and Sony music Entertainment.
It comprises of 17 tracks which comprises of collaborations with ASAKE,SKEPTA,LOGOS OLORI,MUSA KEYS,FAVE,FOCALISTIC,MORRAVEY,DEXTA DAPS,ANGELIQUE KIDJO and THE CAVEMEN
Its production was handled by Magicsticks,Ragee,Darmie,Blaisebeatz,1da Banton,Caltonic SA and Young Alpha
